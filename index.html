Our Story

A Culinary Journey Through Mediterranean Shores

Triton brings together the timeless elegance of Mediterranean cuisine with innovative global influences. Our menu is a celebration of diverse culinary traditions, from authentic Italian pasta and Neapolitan pizza to Indian delicacies and Asian fusion.

Named after the Greek god of the sea, our restaurant embodies the spirit of exploration and the bounty of coastal regions. Each dish is crafted with passion, using the finest ingredients and traditional techniques reimagined for the modern palate.

Step into our beautifully designed space, where bougainvillea blooms overhead and every detail invites you to linger over exceptional food and warm hospitality.
